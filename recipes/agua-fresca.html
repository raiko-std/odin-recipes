Sparkling Cantaloupe Agua Fresca

This refreshing agua fresca with cantaloupe and lime juice topped with club soda is perfect on a hot day.

Cook Time: 15 min

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds fresh cantaloupe cubes (6 cups)
  • 2 cups water
  • 6 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice (from 2 limes)
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups club soda, chilled
  • cantaloupe melon balls or cubes, for garnish
  • lime zest twists, for garnish

Directions:

  1. Working in batches if needed, blend cantaloupe, water, lime juice, honey, and salt in a blender until smooth. Press through a sieve; discard solids. Add strained juice to a pitcher.
  2. Chill until ready to serve. Add club soda just before serving. Serve over ice, and garnish glasses with melon balls and lime zest twists.
